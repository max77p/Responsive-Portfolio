About Me

Hi! Welcome to my page! My name is Shan Indrakumar and I am a developer but definitely not your average one. I come from an Accounting background - 10 years of it! Along the way in my wonderful career in commercial real estate working for Canada's largest REIT, I realized it was time to pursue my passion and learn the language of the future. So at the age of 30, I began sourcing everything I could on the web to teach myself code.

It was a bumpy ride at first because, well, I didn't know where to start. I must admit, I was stuck and lost many times over, but persistence, as with anything in my life, was the key. I remained focused and kept hacking away until I landed on a site called freecodecamp. I began following the freecodecamp curriculum, took some courses on coursera and codeacademy, received great advice from friends and strangers in the industry, and finally developed a clearer picture of where I was heading.

3 years later, I have a strong grasp of HTML and CSS and continue to develop my expertise in Javascript. As I prepare to enter the industry full time, my journey has landed me at one of the best schools in Canada and enrolled in the inaugural Full Stack Development bootcamp. I look forward to this final challenge in my pursuit and am excited about what the future has in store!
